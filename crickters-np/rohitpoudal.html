ROHIT KUMAR PAUDEL

HOME

  • FULL NAME: Rohit Kumar Paudel
  • BORN: September 02, 2002, Nawalparasi
  • BATTING STYLE: Right hand Bat
  • BOWLING STYLE: Right arm Offbreak
  • PLAYING ROLE: Batting Allrounder

Rohit Paudel marked his entry into ODI cricket for Nepal during a match against the Netherlands on August 3, 2018. Remarkably, at the tender age of 15 years and 335 days, he joined the ranks as the fourth-youngest player to debut in an ODI. During his inaugural ODI appearance, he contributed 6 runs off just 15 balls, showcasing his early potential with a boundary. Batting at the number 6 position, Paudel was caught out during the course of the match.

In August 2018, he was named in Nepal's squad for the 2018 Asia Cup Qualifier tournament. In the match against the United Arab Emirates (UAE) on August 30, 2018, Rohit Paudel contributed 9 runs to Nepal's innings. It was his 2nd ODI match. He faced 14 deliveries during his time at the crease, scoring at a strike rate of 64.28. However, he was dismissed after hitting 2 boundaries, caught by the opposition. In October 2018, he was the leading run-scorer for Nepal in the 2018 ACC Under-19 Asia Cup, with 161 runs in three matches.
